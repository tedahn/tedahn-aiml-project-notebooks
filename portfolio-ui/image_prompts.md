# AI/ML Portfolio Image Prompts

Here are some "premium, modern, and colorful" prompts you can use to generate assets. These are tuned for high-quality models (like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion).

## General Style Settings
*Recommended suffix for all prompts to ensure consistency:*
> **... 3d render, isometric style, glassmorphism, vibrant purple and blue gradients, dark slate background, highly detailed, 8k, unreal engine 5 style, soft lighting, tech-minimalism --ar 16:9**

---

## 1. Project Thumbnails (Landscape)

### FoodHub Data Analysis
> **Prompt:** A stylized 3D glass burger and graph chart floating together. Data particles flowing around. Glowing green and orange accents. Food delivery concept meets data science. 3d render, isometric, dark background.

### SuperKart Sales Forecasting
> **Prompt:** A futuristic floating shopping cart with an upward trending stock market line glowing in gold and cyan. Digital retail environment. Fast motion blur. 3d render, glass texture.

### Visa Approval Prediction
> **Prompt:** A glowing holographic passport or ID card with a digital green "approved" checkmark. Global map grid in the background. Cyber security aesthetic. 3d render.

### Renewable Energy Maintenance (ReneWind)
> **Prompt:** Futuristic wind turbines made of white glass and neon lights. Spinning smoothly. Green energy particles. Clean, eco-futuristic look. 3d render, isometric.

### Helmet Detection (HelmNet)
> **Prompt:** A digital construction helmet with a scanning laser grid effect over it. Safety shield icon. Industrial yellow and cyber blue colors. Computer vision concept. 3d render.

### Healthcare QA (GenAI)
> **Prompt:** A DNA helix entwined with a digital circuit board or chat bubble. Medical cross symbol glowing in soft pink and blue. AI healthcare assistant concept. 3d render.
